Random Access Fish ID Key

Tools by Brian Round and programmed by Shawn Morgan

Select the tool to open from the choices in the top menu, either Carps and Minnows or Juvenile Salmoninae. Then to use the tool make selections for key ID features in any order you want; choose a response only for the features you are confident in.

The list will respond to each subsequent selection by reducing itself to possible species for your target specimen.

These tools are designed to accompany Freshwater Fishes of Canada (Scott and Crossman, 1973) and A Field Guide to Freshwater Fishes of Ontario (Holm, Mandrak, and Burridge, 2021). Once you have a short list of potential species, please refer to these books to confirm your answer.

Tip  These can function without an active internet connection. Just open the key page while you do have a connection and keep using it/do not navigate away or refresh the page!

These keys were designed for students of the Fish and Wildlife Technician program at Fleming College's School of Environmental and Natural Resource Sciences, Frost Campus, Lindsay, Ontario. Anyone is welcome to use them, but they will not necessarily be relevant for fish communities outside of Ontario. Find more the programs at https://www.flemingcollege.ca/senrs
